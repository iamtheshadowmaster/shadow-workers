## The ChatGPT Trap: Why General-Purpose AI Fails for OKRs

ChatGPT is amazing at many things. But helping managers and their teams hit quarterly targets isn't one of them.

Here's what actually happens when you try to use ChatGPT for OKR tracking:

### 1. You spend 2+ hours building a "custom GPT"
You create detailed instructions. You add context about your role, your team, your goals. You upload documents. You test different prompts.

**Result**: A ChatGPT instance that works... for you. But your team won't use it because they'd have to do the same setup.

### 2. Your team ignores it
Even if you share your custom GPT, it doesn't understand their specific context. Every team member needs to:
- Re-explain their role
- Re-upload their priorities
- Re-teach it what matters

**Result**: Abandoned tool. Back to spreadsheets.

### 3. It forgets your context
ChatGPT conversations reset. Your OKR progress from last week? Gone. The priorities you set Monday? You'll need to re-explain them on Friday.

**Result**: More work maintaining the tool than just doing the work.

### 4. It doesn't integrate with your workflow
ChatGPT lives in a browser tab. Your work lives in Slack, Calendar, GitHub, Salesforce, Linear.

**Result**: Constant context-switching. Another tool you have to remember to check.
